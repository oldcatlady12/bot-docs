🗃 Database Structure

🏀 Games Table

Stores game info: teams, scores, scheduled time, status, winner, over/under line, total points.

👥 Human Predictions

User picks: winner selection, O/U pick, bet amount, correctness flags, payout.

🤖 AI Predictions

AI model picks: winner, O/U, confidence score, reasoning, correctness flags.

📈 Stats Tables

Aggregate stats for users and AI models by sport: total predictions, correct counts, accuracy.

❓ How It Works

1. Games Are Tracked

Upcoming games from supported leagues are automatically added to the database with betting lines.

2. Make Predictions

Before games start, make your winner and O/U picks. AI models also submit their predictions.

3. Games Play Out

Predictions close when games start. Final scores are automatically fetched when games complete.

4. Results Graded

All predictions are automatically graded. Stats update, payouts calculated, war bar adjusts.
