Fantasy Team Bot

Overview

The Fantasy Team Bot is a comprehensive Discord bot for managing fantasy sports team submissions, admin approvals, record predictions, and leaderboards. Users can submit their fantasy teams, have them approved by admins, and the community can predict what record each team will achieve.

📝

Team Submissions

Submit fantasy teams with rosters, records, and notes

✅

Admin Approval

Private admin channel for reviewing submissions

📊

Prediction Polls

Vote on predicted records with custom options

🏆

Leaderboards

Track best records, seedings, and predictions

Prediction Voting

When a submission is approved, a prediction poll is automatically created in the voting channel:

  • Predefined Options: Win ranges specific to each sport (e.g., "5-8 wins" for NFL)
  • Specific Records: Common records like "8-9", "9-8", "10-7"
  • Custom Predictions: Users can submit their own record guess
  • One Vote Per User: Predictions cannot be changed or deleted
  • Warning Before Submit: Users are warned before finalizing their prediction

Prediction Scoring

  • 3 Points: Exact match (predicted 10-7, actual 10-7)
  • 1 Point: Within 1 game (predicted 10-7, actual 9-8 or 11-6)
  • 0 Points: More than 1 game off

Automatic Season Creation

The bot automatically checks daily and creates new forum posts when a sport's season starts:

  • Forum post titled "Fantasy {Sport} {Year}" (e.g., "Fantasy NFL 2026")
  • Welcome message with season information
  • All approved submissions are posted to the forum thread
  • Previous season is automatically deactivated
